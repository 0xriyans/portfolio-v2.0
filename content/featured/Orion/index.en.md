**ORION** is a digital application designed to revolutionize insurance sales, empowering agents to sell, recruit, and monitor their sales activities directly from their smartphones or tablets. "Digitalize Your Business".

As a backend engineer, I designed a highly resilient, distributed microservices architecture using **Spring Cloud**, **Eureka Server**, and **Zuul Gateway**. I spearheaded the integration of advanced AI engines, deploying automated OCR, biometric Liveness Detection, and Text-to-Speech (TTS) for real-time user verification and accessibility. The system is backed by highly optimized **PostgreSQL** databases, **Apache Kafka** for event-driven asynchronous workflows, and strict **OAuth2/JWT** enterprise-grade security at the gateway layer to protect sensitive financial portals.
