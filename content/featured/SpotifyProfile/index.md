As a Backend Developer for [Fortify](https://fortify.msiglife.co.id/), I helped build a robust digital insurance platform that empowers users to explore, customize, and purchase insurance policies online with ease.

I contributed to building backend services using **Java**, **Spring Boot**, and **MyBatis**, with a microservice architecture supported by **Kafka**, **Oracle DB**, and **Redis** for caching and event-driven processing. Working in an agile environment, I focused on delivering scalable and high-performance APIs, ensuring smooth user interactions and system reliability.

The platform is now live and serves thousands of customers through MSIG Life's digital channels.
