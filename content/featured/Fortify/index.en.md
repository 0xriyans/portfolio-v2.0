**Fortify** is a robust digital life insurance platform that enables users to effortlessly search, customize, and purchase insurance policies online through MSIG Life's digital channels.

As a backend software engineer, I engineered core backend microservices for the public-facing platform using **Java**, **Spring Boot**, and **MyBatis**. I orchestrated extensive historical database migrations and spearheaded the integration of third-party AI engines directly into the submission workflow. By implementing advanced **OCR**, biometric Liveness Detection, and Text-to-Speech (**TTS**) functionalities, I ensured bulletproof onboarding compliance and advanced accessibility, all supported by an event-driven **Kafka** architecture.
